Character: Isaac

Isaac is the main character of the game and the first character you play as.

Isaac's journey is one of self-discovery and overcoming personal demons, which is reflected in the game's dark themes.

Isaac is a reference to the biblical story of the Binding of Isaac, in which Abraham is commanded by God to sacrifice his only son, Isaac, to prove his loyalty and love.

Most characters in the game are actually this Isaac sprite with either a wig, a hat, or some other cosmetic change (he canonically has Dissociative Identity Disorder).

Isaac starts with 3 red heart containers and a bomb.

After progressing through the game to kill Isaac with ??? (Blue Baby), he permanently receives the D6.

Speaking of which, Isaac also shows up as a boss on the Cathedral floor!
