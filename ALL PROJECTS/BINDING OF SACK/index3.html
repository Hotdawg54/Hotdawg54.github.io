Game Breaking Item: Glitched Crown

Glitched Crown is one of the most powerful items you can obtain.

It makes it so that each item pedestal now has 5 items per pedestal.

Each item adheres to the item pool of the room, and each item can be rerolled.

This makes it easier to obtain items you want, items you're looking for, or remove items from a specific pool.

Game Breaking Item: Rock Bottom

Rock Bottom is very powerful. It removes the ability for your stats to go down.

While the item doesn’t change your stats, it makes items like Soy Milk better because it negates the heavy damage reduction while still giving the massive tears increase.

This item also works with active items like Mega Mushroom, which provides a temporary mega stat increase, and with Rock Bottom, it becomes permanent.

Game Breaking Item: Death Certificate

This item is every item—quite literally.

This active item, on use, sends you to a floor with EVERY ITEM IN THE GAME.

You can only take one item, but you can take any item that fits your build properly, making this the most powerful item in the game.
